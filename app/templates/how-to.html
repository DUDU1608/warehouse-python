1) Introduction

This app streamlines warehouse operations: seller/buyer/stockist records, purchases, payments, stock in/out, summaries, and financial reports. Interface available in Hindi (default) and English.

2) Login
  • Admin: Mobile admin, password password
  • User: Registered mobile with OTP verification
  • Role-based access: Modules and data are shown per user role
3) Dashboard

After login, modules appear as per role: Seller, Buyer, Stockist, Stock Summary, Final Report, Income/Expenditure.

4) Seller Module
  • Add Seller: Name, mobile, address, bank
  • Add Purchase: Warehouse, seller, commodity (Wheat/Maize/Paddy), qty, reduction, rate; net qty/total cost auto-calculated
  • Add Payment: Amount & reference; due updates automatically
  • Payment Due: Purchases тИТ Payments
  • Export/Import: PDF/Excel; (Admin) Excel import
  • Inline Edit/Delete: edit/delete directly on the table
5) Stockist Module
  • My Storage: Warehouse/commodity-wise stock (MT)
  • Loans: Summary of Cash Loan & Margin Loan
  • Margin Paid: Total margin paid
  • Stock Exits: RST-based exits; net stock computed
6) Buyer Module

Records for buyers and their purchases/payments. (Admin can add/edit)

7) Stock Summary

Net Stock = Entries тИТ Exits. Displayed separately for Wheat/Maize/Paddy, with warehouse filters.

8) Final Report
  • Warehousing: Rental (At the rate of Rs 3.34 per day per MT) тАФ up to selected date
  • Financing: Interest at 13.75% p.a. on (cash + margin loans тИТ margin paid), summed daily
  • Trading: (to be added)
  • Other Income/Expense: miscellaneous
10) Key Definitions
  • Self Storage: Stored in warehouse; ownership with stockist
  • Company Purchase: Purchased by company and later transferred to the stockist on their request
11) Tips
  • Verify filters before exporting
  • Keep your mobile number updated (used as login)
  • Internet connection is required
